An online article titled "How I Outsource My Self-Control," published on July 2, 2025, and subtitled "And how you can too!", focuses on using commitment devices to enhance productivity. The central message emphasizes that skilled application of these underrated tools, such as app blockers and physical separation from phones, significantly boosts productivity despite common critiques.
