The book cover titled "Get Your House Right" with the subtitle "Architectural Elements to Use & Avoid" is by authors Marianne Cusato and Ben Pentreath, with Richard Sammons & Leon Krier, and features a foreword by H.R.H. The Prince of Wales. The central message of this architectural guide is to provide insight into correct and incorrect elements for house design, as suggested by the title's emphasis on what to "Use & Avoid."
